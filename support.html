❓ Frequently Asked Questions

How can I remove ads?

Our app is free and supported by ads that are safe for children. We plan to offer an ad-free version in a future update. Stay tuned!

Are my child's drawings shared online?

No. All drawings stay only on your device. We do not upload, store, or share any artwork you create.

Why do I see ads if this is for kids?

We show only child-safe, non-personalized ads from Google AdMob. This helps us keep the app free for everyone while respecting children's privacy.

My drawing disappeared. What can I do?

Drawings are saved automatically on your device. If you uninstall the app, they will be lost. Make sure you have enough storage space.

Is personal data collected?

We do not collect any personal information (like name, email, or location). Our ad partner may collect anonymous device data for ad functionality – always respecting COPPA and GDPR child protections.
